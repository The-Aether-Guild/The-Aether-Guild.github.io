The Engineering Lab

Our technical expertise is our greatest asset. We design, build, and deploy our own custom tools to gather data others can't.

Project: The Aether Pod v1.0

The Aether Pod is a networked environmental sensor array designed for continuous data logging. Each pod measures:

  • Ambient Temperature
  • Relative Humidity
  • Barometric Pressure
  • Tri-Axis EMF Fluctuations

This allows us to create a real-time 3D map of an environment's conditions, turning anecdotal feelings of "heavy air" or "cold spots" into measurable data points.

Project: Full-Spectrum Camera Conversion

By removing the internal IR-cut filter from standard DSLR cameras, we enable them to capture light from the near-infrared and near-ultraviolet spectrums, invisible to the human eye.

This technique allows us to capture potential anomalies that exist outside the visible light spectrum, providing a richer, more detailed visual record of our investigations.
