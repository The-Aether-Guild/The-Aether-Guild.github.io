A New Standard of Inquiry

The Aether Guild is a Twin-Cities-based collective dedicated to exploring the paranormal through a unique lens of scientific rigor and open-minded research.

Our Philosophy: Paranormal Engineering

We treat every investigation as an active experiment. Our goal is not just to capture anomalies, but to understand the systems in which they occur. By controlling variables and prioritizing measurable data, we move beyond anecdote and into analysis.

Intuition can tell you where to look; engineering can tell you what you're looking at. We believe both are essential for true discovery.

The Engineering Lab

Our technical expertise is our greatest asset. We design, build, and deploy our own custom tools to gather data others can't.

Featured Project: The Aether Pod v1.0

The Aether Pod is a networked environmental sensor array designed for continuous data logging. Each pod measures:

  • Ambient Temperature
  • Relative Humidity
  • Barometric Pressure
  • Tri-Axis EMF Fluctuations

This allows us to create a real-time 3D map of an environment's conditions, turning anecdotal feelings of "heavy air" or "cold spots" into measurable data points.
